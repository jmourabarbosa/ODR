Progress bars

During the whole trial, two bars will be shown at the top of the screen. The overall progress bar (i.e. how far in the experiment are you) will be shown on the top colored black. Below shown in green, you can see how much money have you made up to this point. Maximum possible reward will be told at the begining of each round.

Reward amounts depend on the number of correct trials sacled by difficulty. For 4 item trial, the reward is 4x higher. Performance only depends on percision around the correct colors.

Formula used to compute rewards

mean((ni / n_max) * ci /ci_max ) where ni = number of items on this trial, n_max = the maximum of number of items on all session, ci = number of catched trials, c_max = total number of catch on this trial.
